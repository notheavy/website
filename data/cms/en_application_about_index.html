Zend\Together - what's that?

First hand informations about ZendFramework - from the community for the community

First hand informations

sessions held by developers who shaped the ZendFramework. From Developers through members of the communityReviewBoard up to the project lead our speakers share their ideas and -Erfahrungen- around the well-known framework

from the community

Out of the germanspeaking ZendFramework-community some mebmers found themselves togehter on a short notice to organise this conference. To share information and knowledge around the ZendFramework is the primary goal.

for the community

A conference for the price of an unconference - that was the main idea to give as many developers from the german speaking area the possibility to broaden their knowledge of ZendFramework.
